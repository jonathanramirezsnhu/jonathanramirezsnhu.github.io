Skills

Driven by my curiosity about the living world, I find myself perpetually immersed in how computer programming (more specifically, data science) can be used to solve the mysteries of biological systems. My current profession as a pathogen lab analyst allows me to have some satisfaction for both fields by testing food for harmful microorganisms using different types of polymerase chain reaction software and then analyzing the results. This daily engagement with the microscopic world fuels my desire to delve deeper into the complexes of biological systems.

My passion extends beyond the lab bench. I am deeply fascinated by the field of computational biology and how it is being used to unravel the mysteries of life and develop innovative solutions to pressing global challenges. The ability to harness the power of computational tools to analyze, model, and simulate biological systems is incredibly exciting to me. I see computational biology as a bridge between my two loves, biology and computer science, and I am eager to contribute to this rapidly evolving field.

Outside of my professional pursuits, I find comfort and stimulation in my hobbies. Reading about characters who endure and preserve inspires me to navigate my own hardships. Tinkering with electronics satisfies my innate desire to understand how things work, while cooking helps me develop my creative side by experimenting with flavors to create delicious and nourishing meals.

I am a lifelong learner, constantly seeking answers to the questions that arise from the world around me. My ultimate goal is to contribute meaningfully to the advancement of computational biology, biotechnology, and bioinformatics, utilizing my skills and passion for discovery to make a positive impact on society.
